Pea soup with noodles and carrots

Ingredients

  • 3,5 l water
  • 1 kg peas
  • 0,25 kg noodles
  • 2 carrots
  • 4 tbs sunflower oil
  • 1 tbs flour
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 0,5 tbs salt
  • 0,5 tbs red pepper

Steps

  1. Heating the oil.
  2. Roasting carrot in the oil for 5 minutes.
  3. Adding water, peas, flour, red pepper, salt and sugar, cooking it for 30 minutes.
  4. Adding noodles, cooking it for 15 minutes,
