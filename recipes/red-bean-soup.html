Red bean soup with sour cream

Ingredients

  • 3,5 l water
  • 0,5 kg red beans
  • 0,5 l sour cream
  • 0,5 tbs salt
  • 0,25 tbs red pepper
  • 0,25 tbs pepper
  • 2 bay leafs
  • 1 onion/serving

Steps

  1. Soaking beans.
  2. Adding water, beans and soy leafs, cooking for 45 minutes.
  3. Adding sour cream, salt, red pepper and pepper, cooking for 15 minutes.
