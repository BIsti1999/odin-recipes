Green bean soup with roux

Ingredients

  • 3,5 l water
  • 1,4 kg green beans
  • 0,5 l roux
  • 0,5 tbs salt

Steps

  • Adding water, beans, salt, cooking for 30 minutes.
  • Preparation of the roux: oil + flour + red pepper + water + sour cream.
  • Adding the roux, cooking for 15 minutes.
